GET YOUR LIFE BACK

with our Full Recovery Focus™

Foothills is where you go for a physical therapy assessment and rehabilitation when you want to make a FULL recovery.

Our Full Recovery Focus™ will help you get your life back because a “good enough” recovery just isn’t good enough.

Stop Hurting, Start Living

Over the last 20 years, we’ve helped over 500,000+ patients with hands-on physical therapy that gets results.

Our Full Recovery Focus™ difference is evident from your very first visit. Your enthusiastic and committed therapist takes you, your pain, and your situation seriously as you discuss what full recovery looks like. Together you will map out a hands-on, customized plan to get you there. No one deserves just a good enough recovery, no matter what their situation may be. Our goal is to do everything we can to help you get your full, healthy life back.

Hands-On Physical Therapy

No two patients or injuries are the same. That’s why your physical therapy experience should be tailored to meet your specific needs.

Cutting Edge Techniques

Therapist continuing education is critical in remaining at the top of our game so you can remain at the top of yours. Our therapists maintain advanced certifications in many specialized services and techniques.

Welcoming Environment

We are built on our daily commitment to our patient’s needs. We don’t work for the insurance carriers. We work for you. We want every patient to feel welcomed and cared for just like they are part of a family.

Dedicated Staff

Your Foothills’ therapist will guide you through the recovery process, work with you, educate you and stick with you until you make a full recovery.

WE ARE A DIRECT ACCESS PROVIDER

Did you know that in Arizona you can be evaluated and treated by a physical therapist without a referral from your physician or healthcare professional? Physical Therapists are an integral part of the health care team and provide a cost-effective access point for patients with a variety of medical and musculoskeletal conditions. Learn more.
